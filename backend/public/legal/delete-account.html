Delete Your NouPro Account

In the app (immediate)

  1. Open NouPro and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Security Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account, read what will be removed, and confirm with your password (and your two-factor code if enabled).

Deletion is immediate and permanent: your profile, connections, and personal data are erased, and all devices are signed out. If you are the only owner of a business that still has other team members, you will be asked to transfer ownership first so your team does not lose access.

What is deleted and what is kept

  • Deleted: your name, photo, contact details, bio, work history, skills, connections, follows, and login credentials.
  • Kept (legal requirement): invoices, orders, and delivery records already exchanged with other businesses remain in their accounting records, no longer linked to an active profile. Your past chat messages show as sent by "Deleted user".

Before you delete

You can download a copy of your personal data first: Settings → Security Settings → Export My Data.

Can't access the app? Email support@nou.pro from the email address on your account and we will process the deletion for you.
